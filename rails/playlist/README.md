# Playlist - Music Sharing Platform

**Version:** 8.0.0  
**Stack:** Rails 8 + Solid Stack + Falcon  
**Port:** Shared with brgen (subdomain)  
**Domain:** playlist.brgen.no (+ all city domains)

## Overview

Music discovery and playlist sharing platform integrated with Brgen's social network.

## Features

- **Create playlists** - Curate your favorites
- **Collaborative playlists** - Build lists with friends
- **Music discovery** - AI-powered recommendations
- **Social sharing** - Share tracks and playlists
- **External integration** - Connect Spotify/Apple Music
- **City charts** - See what your city is listening to
- **Events** - Concert and show listings
- **Artist profiles** - Follow local musicians

cd /home/dev/rails
